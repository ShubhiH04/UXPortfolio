Case study

MarketMate

A grocery planning experience created to help shoppers compare products and make healthier, more informed decisions.

Web app UX Product comparison Responsive design

Challenge

Shoppers often face too many choices and too much detail. Nutrition information can be hard to compare clearly, which slows down decisions.

Approach

I focused on reducing friction by making product information easier to scan, compare, and act on.

What I designed

  • Search and filter flows tailored to dietary needs
  • Readable product comparison patterns
  • Flexible layouts for desktop and mobile screens

Outcome

The project served as a strong example of how clear interaction design can turn a complex shopping task into something more manageable.
