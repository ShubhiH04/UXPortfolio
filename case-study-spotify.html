Case study

Spotify Music Analytics

A research-informed dashboard designed to help teams explore music trends and make faster sense of large streaming datasets.

Dashboard UX Streamlit Data storytelling

Challenge

Spotify datasets are rich but often overwhelming. Teams needed a way to compare streams, genres, popularity, and mood without losing clarity or context.

Approach

I defined the core decision-making tasks first, then structured the interface around comparison, trend review, and quick scanning.

What I designed

  • Clear KPI summaries for quick orientation
  • Side-by-side comparisons for easier pattern recognition
  • Accessible color choices and reduced visual clutter

Outcome

The result was a calmer, more interpretable dashboard that made data exploration feel more intuitive and less overwhelming.
