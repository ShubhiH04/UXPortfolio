Case study

When AI Lies With Confidence

A research-driven concept that helps users evaluate AI-generated health information more critically and confidently.

AI trust Healthcare Research

Challenge

Large language models can sound confident while still being incorrect, especially in sensitive health contexts where trust matters deeply.

Approach

I translated findings into an experience that surfaced uncertainty and supported critical evaluation instead of simply presenting AI output as fact.

What I designed

  • A trust-oriented interface that emphasized evidence and context
  • Clear framing of confidence versus accuracy
  • A product direction grounded in responsible AI thinking

Outcome

The project demonstrated how thoughtful UX can make complex research feel actionable and user-centered.
