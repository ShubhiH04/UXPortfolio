Case study

Tailored

An AI-assisted fashion experience created to reduce decision fatigue and make outfit selection easier for students.

Mobile UX AI recommendations User research

Challenge

Students often make styling decisions under pressure, with limited time, budget, and confidence. The experience needed to feel helpful rather than overwhelming.

Approach

I focused on reducing cognitive load with simple prompts, recommendation logic, and a calm visual structure that feels supportive rather than cluttered.

What I designed

  • Personalized outfit suggestions based on occasion and mood
  • Clear progress through the styling flow
  • A simple interface that made decisions feel less stressful

Outcome

The concept positioned AI as a helpful guide rather than a hard sell, which made the experience feel more human and trustworthy.
