Case study

InterpretAid

A healthcare communication tool designed to help patients and caregivers better understand medical information during stressful visits.

Healthcare UX Accessibility Inclusive design

Challenge

Medical encounters often fail when language, health literacy, or accessibility barriers interfere with understanding. The tool needed to reduce confusion without adding more stress.

Approach

I centered the experience around clarity, trust, and empathy while exploring how support can be delivered in a simple and respectful way.

What I designed

  • Visual and text-based support for complicated terms
  • Accessible and low-friction interactions
  • Patient-centered flows designed around real care moments

Outcome

The work reinforced the importance of designing for vulnerable users with compassion and clarity, not just efficiency.
