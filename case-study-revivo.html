Case study

Revivo

A sustainability-focused app concept that guides users toward repair and reuse rather than replacement.

Sustainability Mobile UX Decision support

Challenge

People often don’t know when repair is the better option, and the decision can feel too complex or too expensive to pursue.

Approach

I shaped a calm and supportive experience with clear options, guided questions, and a simple path toward the next best action.

What I designed

  • Repair-or-replace decision flows
  • Low-friction guidance for everyday users
  • A positive experience that felt practical rather than preachy

Outcome

The experience helped position sustainability as an approachable and useful product goal instead of an abstract mission.
